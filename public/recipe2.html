Plastic Playhouse (Sample Recipe #2)


<< Return to Results



Ingredients

  • Milk Gallon
  • Scissors
  • Stickers(optional)
  • Construction Paper(optional)
  • Glue
  • Markers
  • Paint(optional)


Steps

  1. Use your marker to draw the entrance of your ‘playhouse’! Go wild!
  2. Now add windows(if you want)!
  3. Now the fun-part! Decorate your play-house! Paint it red to look like a fire-station! Or use construction paper to make windowsill! It’s up to you!
